Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles (uncooked)
  • Broccoli florets
  • Slaw dressing (typically made with a combination of vinegar, oil, sugar, and seasonings)
  • Optional toppings or mix-ins such as sesame seeds, sliced almonds, or green onions.

The steps needed for making the Awesome Pasta Salad

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Prepare the vegetables
  3. Make the dressing
  4. Combine ingredients
  5. Add the dressing
  6. Chill and serve
